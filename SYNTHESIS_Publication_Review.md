# SYNTHESIS Multi-Persona Review (Round 8): The Publication Interface

This review evaluates the "Last Mile" of the SYNTHESIS project: turning complex audit data into consumption-ready medical intelligence.

---

## 1. The Journal Editor (BMJ/The Lancet)
**Focus:** Manuscript-Ready Output.

> "The 'Traffic Light' console output is great for the analyst, but I can't print that.
>
> **The Critique:** I need standard tables and figures. Specifically, I need a **'Table 1: The Integrity Audit'** that lists the Core Estimate alongside the Stability Score and Divergence. And I need a **'Figure 1: The Synthesis Plot'** that visually overlays the Density landscape with the Triple-Guard anchors. Currently, I have to piece this together manually."

---

## 2. The Busy Clinician (End-User)
**Focus:** "One-Click" answers.

> "I am not an R programmer. I just want to point this tool at a `.csv` file and get a PDF report on my desktop.
>
> **The Critique:** Please implement a `synthesis_report()` function. It should take my data and generate a formatted **'Clinical Evidence Verification Report'** (Markdown/HTML) that I can attach to my guideline meeting minutes. It must interpret the 'Fail-Safe' decision in plain English."

---

## 3. The Visual Designer (Data Viz Expert)
**Focus:** Cognitive clarity.

> "The current `plot()` function gives us 4 panels. That's good for diagnostics, but we need a **'Summary Visual'**.
>
> **The Critique:** Show me the 'Zone of Trust'. Plot the Effect Size on the X-axis. Show the 'Raw Mean', the 'Standard REML', the 'SYNTHESIS Core', and the 'TGEP Anchor' as points on a line. If they cluster together, it's safe. If they are spread out, visually highlight the 'Divergence Gap' in red."

---

## 4. The Archivist (Reproducibility)
**Focus:** Long-term storage.

> "We are running audits on thousands of datasets. We need to save the *state* of the machine.
>
> **The Critique:** The Audit object should capture the *version* of the package and the *timestamp*. When `synthesis_report()` runs, it should stamp the report with 'Generated by SYNTHESIS v0.1.0 on [Date]'. This is essential for regulatory audit trails."

---

## Final Synthesis Improvement Plan (Round 8)
1.  **New Function**: `synthesis_report()` - Generates a standalone Markdown report file with executive summaries and interpretation.
2.  **Visual Upgrade**: Update `plot.synthesis` to include a "Forest of Methods" (comparing Raw, REML, SYNTHESIS, TGEP).
3.  **Metadata**: Add versioning and timestamps to the Audit object.
